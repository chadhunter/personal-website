Chad Hunter¶


Chad is currently the Director of ESG Modeling at Aspiration where he leads the development and implementation of the ESG methodologies for Aspiration’s ecosystem of consumer and business impact services. Before Aspiration, Chad was the CEO & Co-founder of Carbon Insights and a Team Lead at the U.S. DOE National Renewable Energy Laboratory.
